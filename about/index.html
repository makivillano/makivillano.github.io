About me

I am Maki Villano and I tinker with all sorts of things.

My background is in electronics engineering but I found myself spending the past fifteen years in software development, taking on various roles from R&D to consulting to management. I spent my first decade working for companies such as HP, Misys, Oracle, and Gameloft followed by several years of trying to launch a few tech startups. Currrently, I am an independent technology consultant.

Outside of consulting, I continue to tinker, learn, create, teach, and play. I presently reside in the wonderful islands of Philippines with my amazing son Io.
